Our Training Program

 

We limit the number of pups we have in for training at any one time. Allowing us to give each pup the time and attention it deserves. Your pup's daily routine includes walks, playtime with other Goldens/dogs, interactions with people as well as actual training.

Your pup learns basic commands such as sit, down, stay, come, wait at a door. We teach the pup proper crate manners and begin house breaking.

Your pup will be introduced to the the big wide world. By attending pup training classes and daycare off the property. Included are car rides to visit downtown Fort Collins where we head into stores such as Home Depot, Ace Hardware etc.

We believe all these interactions are the formula to making a Canine Good Citizen.

Basic program is 1-2 months. We do offer a "crash" course of 2 weeks.
