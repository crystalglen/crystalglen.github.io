New Owners

Peri Fucetola

 

Expectations: First and foremost is to have a trust between the new owner and us at Crystal Glen. We consider the purchase of a Crystal Glen Golden Retriever to be a long term relationship and we want it to be a good one!

I expect my puppies to be part of the family.  Spending time with the family on a daily basis, not just put out in the backyard as a lawn ornament. The dog needs to have a confined area, yard, electric fence, run etc.; where it can be safely and properly housed out of doors. The puppies must be trained so that they are good members of their families. (See below) Spaying/neutering is also required. 

Puppy Lists: My pet/companion/therapy quality pups are sold on a first come first serve basis.  This is decided by deposit, who ever puts their deposit down first gets first pick of the pet pups from a litter.  The deposit required is a non-refundable $200.00. A deposit is accepted after we have a chance to meet. 

Price: Do to the dedication and the effort put forth with each breeding. Along with the fact that our Golden Retrievers have accomplished more than any other in the Rocky Mountain region. And that 90% of my clients become repeat owners - all of this speaks volumes to the quality of Golden Retriever we produce and is reflected in the price of the pup.

What we are about: Crystal Glen has been breeding Golden Retrievers in Colorado for close to 45 years. . We have only 1 to 3 litters per year. Our commitment to our puppies is unsurpassed. During the first 8 weeks we offer the pups a world of possibilities. To develop their minds, bodies, and personalities. So when the pup goes home it has the necessary foundations to become a good citizen.

Philosophy: to get a true understanding of our breeding program.

Puppy Training. Is a must. If you need help, we have that option available at  Crystal Glen .
