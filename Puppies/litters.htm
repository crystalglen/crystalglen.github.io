Our Golden Retriever Breeding Program   

Bristol owned by: Deb Ascher and Crystal Glen

 

Crystal Glen has been breeding Golden Retrievers in Colorado for over forty years. We have only 1 to 3 litters per year.Our commitment to our puppies is unsurpassed. During the first 8 weeks we offer the pups a world of possibilities. To develop their minds, bodies, and personalities.
